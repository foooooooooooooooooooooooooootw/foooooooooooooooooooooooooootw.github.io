Chatuchak

On the final days of twenty four,

I chanced across a peculiar store;

Discarded jewelry in crates galore.

Precious stones set no more.

Box on box I sifted thru,

Till I found these pieces two.

And then a spark within my brain;

I saw them both - born again.
