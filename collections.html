My Handmade Creations

Sun & Moon

A striking pair of opposites, captured in silver and brass. The sunstone, with its vibrant orange hue, sits in a rectangular band adorned with vertical prongs that radiate like sunrays.

In contrast, the moonstone's soft blue glow rests in an oval setting, its band intricately designed with cutouts that create delicate gaps, evoking the quiet, open beauty of the night.

One band found in a bustling flea market, the other imported from across the sea — yet together, they mirror the eternal dance of day and night. When light strikes them, each stone reveals its own luminous character.

Chatuchak

On the final days of twenty four,

I chanced across a peculiar store;

Discarded jewelry in crates galore.

Precious stones set no more.

Box on box I sifted thru,

Till I found these pieces two.

And then a spark within my brain;

I saw them both - born again.
