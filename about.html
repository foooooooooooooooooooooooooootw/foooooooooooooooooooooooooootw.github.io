Who am I?

I'm T'suen Wei - A music, analytics, software/hardware, firearm & gem enthusiast.

In my free time, I enjoy refurbishing hardware, programming, writing, playing music and creating jewelry.


Background

My family and upbringing was by no means regular. My parents were lawyers; their words were used to corner and tear down that their adversaries. After years of this, I had eventually realized if I did the opposite and paired it with a smile I could make fast friends with anyone. Fast forward into the present day, I have friends all over SEA, with some even beyond that in Europe and the Americas.
