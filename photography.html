About the Artist

Welcome to my photography portfolio. I'm passionate about capturing the beauty of our world through my lens, with a special focus on sky photography and urban landscapes.

Photography allows me to express my perspective and share the fleeting moments that often go unnoticed in our busy lives. Each image tells a story and freezes a moment in time that can never be precisely recreated.

My work spans various styles and subjects, but I'm particularly drawn to the ever-changing canvas of the sky, and older architecture which I think are quickly being destroyed and replaced with glass towers.

I try my best to take photos that are "fantasy-like", and photos that don't include people to create a sense of liminality or isolation.

The art of taking photos itself is very personal to me. I was born with Antimetropia and Astigmatism in both eyes, and photography is the only way I think I can see the world as other people do.

I hope my images inspire you to look around more often and appreciate the art show happening every day.
